Explore Bangalore

SS Crown Inn is ideally located to explore the vibrant city of Bangalore. Here are some nearby attractions:

  • Lal Bagh Botanical Garden: A beautifully landscaped garden perfect for a stroll.
  • Cubbon Park: A large green space in the heart of the city, ideal for relaxation.
  • Bangalore Palace: A historical palace reflecting Tudor and Scottish Gothic architecture.
  • UB City: A luxury mall for high-end shopping and dining.
  • MG Road: A popular commercial hub with shops, restaurants, and entertainment.
